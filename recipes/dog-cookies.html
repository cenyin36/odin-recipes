Dog Cookies

This is a recipe to make delicious cookies for dogs

Prep time: 15 mins

Bake time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup - pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup - peanut butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. Attach paddle attachment to electric mixer
  4. Add pumpkin puree, peanut butter and eggs to bowl of electric mixer
  5. Beat combination on medium high until thoroughly mixed
  6. Switch to low speed and slowly add 2 1/2 cups flow
  7. Continue until flour becomes slightly incorporated
  8. Add remaining flour slowly until dough is no longer sticky
  9. Remove dough and place on lightly floured surface
  10. Knead dough until it comes together
  11. With rolling pin, roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness
  12. Use cookie cutters to create shapes and place onto lined baking sheet
  13. Bake for 30 mins or until edges are golden brown
  14. Let cool for 15 mins
