Grilled Cheese Sandwich

This is a recipe to make a restaurant style grilled cheese sandwich

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium low heat
  2. Generously butter one side of a bread slice
  3. Place bread slice butter-side-down on skillet
  4. Add single piece of cheese to slice
  5. Let sit in skillet for 5 mins, until cheese starts to melt
  6. Remove slice, set aside and cover with tin foil
  7. Repeat steps 3 - 6 with remainder of bread slices
  8. Place one slice back in skillet, add additional slice on top
  9. Cook until both bread slices golden brown, flipping occasionally
  10. Repeat steps 8 and 9 with additional bread slices
